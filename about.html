Hi, I’m Sean Valenzuela.

I’m a UX/UI designer/ developer with loads of indispensable business experience and skills. I developed these skills, thanks to five years in the financial industry where I developed a talent for efficiency and accuracy.


I went through the rigorous courses at the University of Arizona and graduated with my bachelors degree in marketing. I then worked for an investment management company in Phoenix where I honed my communication skills in a collaborative team environment.


After six years of the Phoenix heat, I decided to move back to Tucson to be closer to family and I resolved to make a career change to something I’m more passionate about. Whether it’s creating this years Halloween costume or planning out my next woodworking project, I’ve always been interested in design.


When I moved back, I made the leap and I took a comprehensive web design course through Thinkful. I learned about interesting topics like ux/ui, visual design, and frontend development. I love what I do and I’m excited to hit the ground running as a web designer here in Tucson.
